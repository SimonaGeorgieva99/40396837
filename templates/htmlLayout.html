<header> - A header for a website, most common thing included there is the title.
<nav> - A navigation bar containing links to other pages from the website.
<section> - A section of a document which is part from the body.
<article> - Another part of the body - content of a document.
<aside> - Something like a sidebar, content aside from the main one.
<footer> - Footer of the document.
